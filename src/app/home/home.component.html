Why The Charles?

The name of the platform takes inspiration from the Charles River in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Literally speaking, the idea of the platform was conceived inside a Harvard dormitory overlooking river. As the river was flowing, we gazed outside of our window and watched strangers, friends, colleagues, and classmates pass by. We realized that there are many stories that are continuously being produced, yet solemnly shared. So, for a moment, we want to stop this current and cherish it for all that it is.
